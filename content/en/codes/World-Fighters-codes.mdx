## Understanding Reward Types

In World Fighters, not all rewards are created equal. Depending on your current level, you may find certain types of **World Fighters codes** more valuable than others. Understanding the game's economy will help you decide when to use your boosts for maximum efficiency.

### Gems vs. Coins
Gems are the premium currency used for high-tier summons and permanent character upgrades. Coins, while more common, are essential for basic level-ups and purchasing consumable items from the in-game shop.

### Luck and XP Boosts
These are timed items. You should never activate a Luck Boost unless you have enough Gems saved up to perform a large number of summons. Activating a 30-minute boost and then only doing one summon is a waste of a valuable resource.

## Troubleshooting Your Codes

Occasionally, you might encounter an error message when trying to use **World Fighters codes**. This is common and usually stems from one of a few specific issues. Before giving up, verify the following:

*   **Case Sensitivity:** "FIGHTER" is not the same as "fighter." Ensure your Caps Lock key is set correctly.
*   **Trailing Spaces:** If you copy and paste a code, you might accidentally include a space at the end. The system will read this as an invalid character.
*   **Expired Status:** Most codes only last for 1 to 4 weeks. If a code is from a 2025 update, it is likely no longer valid in 2026.
*   **One-Time Use:** Codes can only be redeemed once per account. Attempting to use the same code on an alt account is fine, but you cannot "double-dip" on your main profile.
