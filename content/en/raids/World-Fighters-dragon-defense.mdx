## Essential Defensive Structures

In **World Fighters dragon defense**, your stationary assets are just as vital as your mobile ones. A common mistake among beginners is over-investing in aircraft while leaving their hangars and missile silos vulnerable to low-altitude strikes. Building a "layered defense" is the most reliable way to ensure survival during the late-game "Hell Gates" waves.

### Tier 1: Perimeter Control
The first line of defense should always consist of rapid-fire AA guns and short-range SAM (Surface-to-Air Missile) sites. These are designed to shred incoming projectiles and distract enemy drones.

### Tier 2: Heavy Deterrence
The middle layer should house your ballistic missile bases. In the 2026 update, underground military structures have received a significant durability buff. Placing your silos deep within mountain biomes or using "fortification" upgrades can protect them from the "Mother of All Bombs" (MOAB) strikes that elite AI bosses frequently use.

## Tactical Scenarios: Countering the Displacement Plan

One of the hardest missions in the 2026 campaign is the "Counter-Displacement" scenario. In this level, the enemy attempts to force your civilian units out of the sector while using heavy "Mother of All Bombs" payloads to destroy your underground fortifications.

To succeed, you must prioritize the destruction of the enemy's heavy bombers. Use your Great Dragon fleet to engage at the 400km mark. If the bombers get within 100km, your stationary defenses will likely be overwhelmed by the blast radius of their ordinance.
