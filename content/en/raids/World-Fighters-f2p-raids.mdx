The 1.75 update, often referred to as Part Three of the recent content cycle, focuses heavily on streamlining the player experience. For those focusing on World Fighters f2p raids, these changes are transformative. One of the most significant additions is the **Auto Claim Rewards** feature. Previously, players had to manually interact with reward menus, which interrupted the flow of AFK grinding. ## Mechanics of World Fighters f2p raids

To excel in World Fighters f2p raids, you must understand the specific requirements and rewards for each encounter. The game currently features several key modes: Dragon Defense, Slime Invasion, and Tempest Invasions. Each offers a unique set of rewards that are essential for different stages of your character's growth.

### Dragon Defense
Dragon Defense remains one of the most consistent ways to farm tokens. Players typically spend a night in the Dragon Arena to accumulate keys, which are then used to enter the defense mode. With the 1.75 update, you can now apply a **2x Speed Modifier** by consuming an additional key. This doubles the movement speed of enemies, effectively cutting the time required to complete the raid in half.

### Slime Invasion
The Slime Invasion is the primary source for accessories like the Admiral Cape, Cute Slime, and Fire Hat. While these drops have varying rarities, the mode also provides a 50% chance for two shards upon completion. For F2P players, this is the most reliable method to farm the shards needed for crafting secret-tier equipment.

### Tempest Invasions
Tempest Invasions are high-difficulty encounters that require significant power levels to clear effectively. The 1.75 update fixed an annoying bug where players were not teleported back to their original location after completion. This fix is vital for players using macros to cycle through invasions while they are away from their computers.

## Optimizing Speed Modifiers

The introduction of speed modifiers has changed the calculation for "optimal grinding." While the 2x speed modifier is highly recommended for any player with a surplus of keys, the 3x speed modifier is currently locked behind a gem cost. For most F2P players, the 2x speed offers the best balance of efficiency and resource management.

| Modifier Level | Cost | Recommended For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1x (Normal)** | Standard Entry Key | Beginners or low-DPS builds. |
| **2x Speed** | **1 Extra Entry Key** | Mid-to-late game F2P players. |
| **3x Speed** | Gems | Players with excess premium currency. |

When using the 2x speed modifier in World Fighters f2p raids, the enemies move significantly faster toward your base. It is imperative that your units have enough range and damage to clear the waves before they can breach your defenses. If you find yourself failing raids at 2x speed, it is a sign that you need to focus on upgrading your unit passives or acquiring better swords from the gacha.

## The Ultimate AFK Grinding Strategy

For a free-to-play player, the most effective way to progress is to utilize AFK (Away From Keyboard) strategies. By setting up a macro or using the built-in auto-features, you can accumulate resources while not actively playing.

1.  **Key Accumulation:** Spend time in the Dragon Arena or Slime World to farm entry keys. A full night of AFK farming in the Dragon Arena can yield dozens of keys.
2.  **Raid Cycling:** Use your keys in the Dragon Defense or Slime Invasion. Ensure "Auto" is enabled and, if you have the keys to spare, toggle the 2x speed modifier.
3.  **Token Spending:** Take your earned tokens to the Gacha. With the new "Hide Gacha Animation" setting, you can burn through thousands of tokens in seconds.
4.  **Unit Upgrading:** Focus on reaching the **Secret Rarity** for all your units. The 1.75 update makes this easier by providing more consistent token drops from the sped-up raids.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When macroing, ensure your character is positioned in a way that allows your units to cover all entry points. A slight misplacement can lead to a failed raid and wasted keys.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the 2x speed modifier worth using for World Fighters f2p raids?**

A: Yes, provided you have a steady supply of keys. It effectively doubles your resource gain per hour, which is essential for F2P players looking to keep up with the power creep.

**Q: Where can I find the new Damage Test Dummy?**

A: The Damage Test Dummy is located directly in front of the leaderboards in the main hub. It has infinite health, allowing you to accurately measure your DPS and the effectiveness of different unit compositions.

**Q: How do I get more keys for raids without paying?**

A: Keys are dropped by enemies in the various adventure worlds. The most efficient method is to set your character to auto-farm in the Dragon Arena or Slime World overnight.

**Q: Does the auto-claim rewards feature reset automatically?**

A: No. While it automatically claims the rewards as you earn them, you still need to manually reset the reward track once it is completed to start a new cycle.
