## Understanding Secret Rarity and Unit Tiers

In World Fighters, "Secret" isn't just a label for hidden characters; it is a distinct rarity tier that sits above Mythic. While most units can be obtained through the standard gacha system, reaching the secret tier usually involves significant grinding or extreme luck during specific game modes. The power gap between a standard Mythic and a secret unit is substantial, often featuring unique animations and higher scaling modifiers.

| Rarity Tier | Drop/Craft Difficulty | Power Multiplier | Special Features |

## How to Farm Baron Naruto and Trial Secrets

One of the most sought-after **World Fighters secret units** is Baron Naruto. Unlike units found in the standard gacha stars, Baron Naruto is tied to the Trial system. Specifically, he is a rare drop from the "Trial Easy" mode, though his actual drop percentage remains hidden behind a question mark in the game's UI.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Even with high-efficiency macros, Baron Naruto is notoriously difficult to obtain. Some players report 24-hour AFK sessions without a single drop.

To improve your efficiency when farming trials:
1.  **Utilize the Damage Dummy:** Before starting a farm, visit the new test dummy located in front of the leaderboards. Ensure your DPS is peaking high enough to clear waves instantly.
2.  **Enable Hide Gacha Animations:** This new quality-of-life feature saves seconds per pull, which adds up to hours over a long farming session.
3.  **Check Your Teleport Settings:** Ensure you are using the latest update feature that teleports you back to your last position upon quitting or finishing a game mode.

Farming these requires "Slime Keys" or "Sayan Keys." A major update in 1.75 introduced speed modifiers. You can now spend a key to increase the game speed to 2x, or spend Gems for 3x speed. This is essential for clearing the 20 waves required for the highest-tier rewards.

## Optimizing Your Grind with Speed Modifiers

The introduction of speed modifiers has revolutionized how players hunt for **World Fighters secret units**. Previously, a full run of Dragon Defense could take a significant amount of time, making it difficult to farm enough tokens for secret gacha upgrades.

To maximize this feature:
*   **Key Management:** Farm the Dragon Arena first to stockpile keys.
*   **2x Speed:** Always use 2x speed when using keys. It effectively doubles your token-per-hour rate without costing premium currency.
*   **3x Speed:** Reserved for players with an abundance of Gems. This is the fastest way to max out your secret gacha units.

> **💡 Tip:** One and a half nights of AFK farming in Dragon Defense with 2x speed is typically enough to max out all secret gacha tokens for a specific world.

## Quality of Life Changes for Secret Hunters

The 1.75 update was specifically designed to help players who are grinding for high-end content. Several new features make managing your inventory of **World Fighters secret units** much easier:

3.  **Passive Selection:** If you have crafted a passive with a 100% chance, you can now use the "Select" button to apply it immediately, streamlining the unit customization process.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get World Fighters secret units?**

A: The fastest method is to farm keys in the Dragon Arena and then run the Dragon Defense mode on 2x or 3x speed. This allows you to accumulate tokens quickly, which can be used to pull for secret units in the gacha.

**Q: How do I find Baron Naruto?**

A: Baron Naruto is a secret drop from the Easy Trial mode. He has an extremely low drop rate, and his odds are currently hidden (displayed as a question mark). Most players use macros to farm this mode overnight.

**Q: Do speed modifiers affect drop rates?**

A: Speed modifiers do not change the percentage chance of a drop, but they allow you to complete waves and trials much faster, effectively increasing the number of "rolls" you get per hour for **World Fighters secret units**.

**Q: Can accessories reach secret rarity?**

A: Yes, accessories like the Admiral Cape and Fire Hat can reach secret rarity. These are typically obtained through shards and tokens found in Invasion modes like the Slime Invasion.
