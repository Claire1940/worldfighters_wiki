## Champions and Rarity Tiers

The Champion Gacha is perhaps the most critical component of the **World Fighters gacha guide** for players aiming for the leaderboard. Champions act as pets or companions that provide a passive multiplier to your training efficiency.

Rolling for champions requires Chakra Shards. While basic champions provide a modest 1.5x or 2x boost, the high-tier "Red" rarity champions can offer astronomical improvements to your per-minute gains.

1. **Common/Rare Tiers**: These are easy to obtain and should be your first goal. Even a Rare tier champion is substantially better than training without one.
2. **Legendary Tiers**: These usually have a drop rate below 1%. They are essential for mid-game progression.
3. **Saitama Series (Special)**: The rarest champions in the game. To reliably pull these, you may need upwards of 12 million Chakra Shards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend all your shards on the first champion gacha you see. Save your resources for the late-game gachas (like the Saitama series) where the multipliers are significantly higher.
