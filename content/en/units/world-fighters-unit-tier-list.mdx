## Upgrade Path: What to Do in Order

If your account feels stuck, follow this sequence:

1. **Stabilize one carry from the top of the world fighters unit tier list**
2. **Improve Tempest upgrades** (range first, then speed/damage)
3. **Farm keys consistently** before gambling heavy
4. **Use mythics as transitional units**, not final investments
5. **Shift to secret hunting only after invasion consistency improves**

This order works because it keeps your gains compounding. Every step increases the value of the next one, instead of resetting your progress loop.

> **Quick rule:** If you cannot farm invasion efficiently, your next best move is usually account upgrades—not replacing your whole team.
