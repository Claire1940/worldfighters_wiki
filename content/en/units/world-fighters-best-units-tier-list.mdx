## Essential Power Scaling Systems

While having the best units is vital, World Fighters Simulator relies heavily on secondary systems to multiply the base stats of those units. To truly utilize the **world fighters best units tier list**, you must engage with Awakening, Passives, and Hockey.

### The Awakening Multiplier
Awakening is arguably the most broken mechanic in the game. By turning on "Auto-Awaken," you can cycle through buffs that eventually reach astronomical numbers. The maximum tier of Awakening can grant a multiplier of over 8 million times, which completely dwarfs any individual unit's base stats.

### Passives and Divine Energy
Every unit on your team can be further enhanced with passives. The most sought-after passive is **Divine Energy**.
1. Navigate to the Passive station.
2. Select your main character.
3. Combine 10 Wooden Katanas and 6 Diamonds.
4. This results in a 100% success rate for a 1.55x power boost.

## Accessories: The Secret to Infinite Damage

Accessories are often overlooked by casual players, but they are what "carry" you through the Time Trials. Every secret boss in the game has a chance to drop an accessory for a specific slot (Back, Body, Head, Mask, or Waist).

| Accessory Slot | Best Source | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Back** | Secret Bosses | Flat Damage Buff |
| **Body** | Event Game Modes | % Power Increase |
| **Head** | New World Mobs | Attack Speed + Power |
| **Waist** | Time Trial Rewards | Multi-stat Scaling |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore the "Wanted" posters at the start of each map. Completing these is the only way to unlock the specific units mentioned in the S-Tier rankings.

## Advanced Optimization: Renaming and Titles

A unique quirk of World Fighters Simulator is the ability to rename units for stat bonuses. For example, renaming a unit like Naruto can trigger a hidden multiplier. Aim for at least a 1.6x multiplier, though the maximum achievable is 1.75x power and 1.7x damage. This is a "luck-based" mechanic, so players often spam names until the desired stats appear.

### Top-Tier Titles for Power
Titles are not just for show; they provide passive buffs that apply to your entire team.
* **Dual Blade Title**: Currently considered the best for raw power.
* **Drop Rate Titles**: Essential for farming secret accessories from bosses.
* **Luck Titles**: Best used when opening Gacha stars to find Secret-tier units.

## Farming the Time Trial

The Time Trial is the ultimate testing ground for your units. It is the primary source of Crystals, which are required to max out your Attack Speed and Damage. 

**Recommended Time Trial Progression:**
1. **Focus on Power first**: You cannot clear rooms if you can't one-tap the mobs.
2. **Attack Speed second**: Once your power is sufficient, speed becomes the bottleneck for clearing rooms within the time limit.
3. **Damage third**: This helps with boss encounters within the trial.

Reaching Room 50 is a common milestone that signifies your team has moved into the "Overpowered" (OP) category. At this stage, you should be able to AFK farm most world bosses with ease.

## FAQ

**Q: Which unit should I focus on first in the world fighters best units tier list?**

A: For beginners, Ace is the priority. He is relatively easy to obtain in the One Piece world and provides a significant enough boost to reach the later worlds where Diablo and Katakuri are available.

**Q: Is the 2x Power game pass worth it for free-to-play players?**

A: While helpful, it is not strictly necessary. Using the "Remote Stars" pass is often better for F2P players because it allows you to AFK kill and summon simultaneously, which leads to more Secret units and accessories over time.

**Q: How do I get Divine Energy on my units?**

A: You must go to the Passive reroll area and use 10 Wooden Katanas and 6 Diamonds. This ensures a 100% chance to roll Divine Energy, granting your unit a 1.55x power multiplier.

**Q: Does renaming my character actually increase my damage?**

A: Yes, renaming is a core mechanic. By changing your character's name, you can roll for random stat bonuses, with the ceiling being approximately 1.75x power. It is a cheap way to squeeze extra performance out of your best units.
