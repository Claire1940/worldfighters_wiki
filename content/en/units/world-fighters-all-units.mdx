## world fighters all units: How to Evaluate Any Unit Fast

A strong unit is not just “high damage.” In World Fighters, value comes from five factors working together: DPS uptime, AoE shape, placement flexibility, cost efficiency, and scaling with upgrades/traits. Use this checklist before investing.

| Evaluation Factor | What to Check | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **DPS Uptime** | Attack interval + animation lock + retarget speed | Consistent damage beats burst-only units in long waves |
| **AoE Pattern** | Cone, circle, line, full AoE, or single target | Wave clear depends on AoE geometry, not only raw numbers |
| **Placement Value** | Ground vs hill vs hybrid range options | Flexible units fit more maps and reduce dead slots |
| **Cost Curve** | Initial cost vs full-upgrade total | Expensive carries can stall early waves if unsupported |
| **Scaling** | Upgrade multipliers, trait synergy, passives | Good scaling keeps units relevant in late waves and raids |

### Quick Role Labels You Should Use
When comparing world fighters all units, classify each unit into one of these roles first:
