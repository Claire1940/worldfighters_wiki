## Quality of Life: Streamlining the Grind

The 1.75 update is heavily focused on removing the "friction" from daily gameplay. One of the most requested features was a way to manage the massive influx of items without accidental deletions. The developers have finally added a confirmation prompt when deleting swords, units, and accessories. This safety net is essential when you are managing a vault full of Mythic and Secret rarities.

Additionally, the gacha system has received a significant speed boost. Players can now toggle off the gacha summon animation entirely. This allows for rapid-fire summoning sessions, which is the most effective way to hunt for the elusive Secret units in the current meta.

| Feature | Change Description | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Auto-Claim Rewards** | Rewards are now automatically collected as they become available. | Saves time during long AFK sessions. |
| **Hide Gacha Animation** | Completely skips the summoning sequence for units. | Allows for faster unit cycling and inventory filling. |
| **Drop Billboards** | Displays potential drops directly next to game mode portals. | Players no longer need to check external wikis for drop tables. |
| **Delete Confirmation** | Adds a "Are you sure?" prompt for high-value items. | Prevents accidental loss of Secret or Mythic gear. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep "Hide Gacha Animation" enabled when you have a large stock of tokens. It can cut your summoning time down by nearly 80%, allowing you to find **World Fighters new units** much faster.

## Speed Modifiers and Invasion Efficiency

One of the most impactful additions in 2026 is the introduction of speed modifiers for Dragon Defense and Slime Invasions. These modifiers allow players to accelerate the waves of enemies, effectively doubling or tripling the speed of token acquisition.

However, these modifiers come at a cost. While 2x speed can be activated using specific keys (like Slime Keys or Saiyan Keys), 3x speed currently requires Gems. For players looking to max out their Secret gachas, the 2x speed modifier is the "sweet spot" for overnight AFK grinding.

| Modifier Level | Cost Requirement | Recommended Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1x Speed** | Free (Default) | Standard gameplay and testing new builds. |
| **2x Speed** | 1x Mode Key (e.g., Slime Key) | Overnight AFK farming for tokens and shards. |
| **3x Speed** | Gems | Rapid farming when pushing for leaderboard spots. |

## World Fighters New Units and Secret Rarities

While 1.75 is a QoL-heavy update, the hunt for **World Fighters new units** remains the core focus for the community. The "Secret" rarity continues to be the gold standard for power, with units like Baron Naruto and Diablo dominating the meta.

The drop rates for these units remain hidden (indicated by the infamous "?" in the UI), but community testing suggests that the Trial mode is the most reliable place to find them. Players have reported that even with a full night of macroing, Baron Naruto remains incredibly rare, emphasizing the need for the new speed modifiers to increase the number of attempts per hour.

### Current Top-Tier Units to Watch
1. **Baron Naruto**: The current king of DPS, found in the Trial Easy/Hard modes.
2. **Diablo**: A powerhouse unit that synergizes well with the latest sword animations.
3. **Secret Slime Units**: Obtained through the Slime Invasion shards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When macroing the Trial mode, ensure your "Auto-Sell" settings are configured correctly. With the new speed modifiers, your inventory will fill up much faster than in previous versions.

## Optimizing DPS with the Test Dummy

For the first time, players can accurately measure their power using the Damage Test Dummy located in front of the leaderboards. This dummy has infinite health and provides a real-time DPS (Damage Per Second) readout.

This is crucial for testing **World Fighters new units** and determining which passives are actually contributing to your clear speed. With the new "Select Button" for 100% crafted passives, you can now swap between different builds and immediately see the numerical difference on the dummy.

### How to Use the Test Dummy Effectively
*   **Equip your best accessories**: Items like the Admiral Cape or Fire Hat can provide hidden multipliers.
*   **Check your range**: If your units are too far from the dummy, their DPS will drop. Use this to find the "sweet spot" for your unit placement.
*   **Test Passive Synergies**: Some passives look good on paper but underperform in practice. The dummy provides the objective truth.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the new World Fighters new units like Baron Naruto?**

A: Baron Naruto is a rare secret drop from the Trial modes. While the exact odds are hidden, most players obtain him by using an auto-clicker or macro to farm the Trial Easy mode overnight.

**Q: Is the 3x speed modifier worth the Gem cost?**

A: For most players, 2x speed is sufficient for general progression. 3x speed is recommended only if you are in a rush to max out a specific unit's shards before a major update or if you have an abundance of Gems.

**Q: How do I access the new Slime Invasion accessories?**

A: You must participate in the Slime Invasion game mode. There is a chance to obtain the Admiral Cape, Cute Slime, and Fire Hat as rewards. These are currently some of the strongest accessories for boosting your units' stats.

**Q: What happened to the enemy attack delay?**

A: This was a bug in previous versions where enemies in dungeons would have a delay before attacking. The 1.75 update has fixed this, meaning combat is now faster and more responsive, but also slightly more dangerous if your units have low health.
