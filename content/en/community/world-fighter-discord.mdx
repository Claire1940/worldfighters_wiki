## How to join world fighter discord safely and set up your account

When players ask how to get started, the biggest issue usually isn’t joining—it’s joining *safely*. Impersonation links and fake “staff DMs” are still common in 2026 across many gaming communities.

### Step-by-step safe onboarding

1. Start from trusted game-facing links (official game profile, official social pages, or in-game announcements).

Use this setup checklist:

## Rules, etiquette, and troubleshooting in world fighter discord

Good reputation in a game Discord matters. Players remember reliable teammates and fair traders. A few etiquette basics can dramatically improve your experience:

- Don’t spam pings for one run.
- Post complete trade terms (item, quantity, price range).
- Confirm party expectations before starting.
- Share evidence calmly in dispute channels.
- Read channel topic lines to avoid off-topic moderation hits.

### Common problems and fixes

| Problem | Likely Cause | Fast Fix |
|---|---|---|
| “Can’t type in channels” | Verification or role not complete | Re-check rules + verify channel steps |
| “Not seeing LFG channels” | Missing role selection | Assign region/activity roles |
| “Too many notifications” | Default all-message alerts | Set mentions-only + mute low-priority channels |
| “Got suspicious DM” | Scam wave targeting new users | Block/report sender, avoid external links |
| “No one replies to group posts” | Vague LFG request | Add objective, level, time, and region |
| “Trade arguments” | No clear terms/screenshots | Use structured trade template with proof |

If moderation actions happen, avoid arguing in public channels. Use support/ticket channels with concise evidence and timestamps.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never move a trade to untrusted external platforms when a server has established trade procedures. Keep transactions documented.
