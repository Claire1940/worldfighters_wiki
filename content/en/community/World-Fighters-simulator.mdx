## Understanding the Quest System Updates

The 2026 updates have introduced several quality-of-life (QOL) improvements that make the grind significantly more manageable. One of the most important additions is the **Auto-Claim** feature. Previously, players had to manually return to NPCs to hand in completed objectives. Now, once you meet the requirements for a side quest, the rewards are automatically applied to your character profile, allowing for seamless AFK grinding sessions.

To optimize your progress, always ensure your "Auto-Claim" toggle is set to "On" in the quest menu. This is particularly vital for long-term objectives involving thousands of kills, as it prevents any downtime between completing one milestone and starting the next.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check your quest log before starting an AFK session. Some quests require you to roll for specific units or items that can only be done while you are actively at the summoning altars.

## Side Quest NPC Locations and Objectives

Finding NPCs in the vast zones of the game can be challenging. Below is a detailed breakdown of the primary side quests available in the current version of the game.

### Quest 1: The Initial Power Boost
Located in the starting zone, this is the first major hurdle for new players. You need to locate a specific building near the spawn area. Go inside to find the NPC waiting in a small room.

| Objective | Requirement | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mob Kills** | Beat enemies 750 times | **1.5x Power Multiplier** |
| **Zone Access** | Starting Island | Permanent Stat Buff |

This quest is fundamental because the 1.5x power multiplier stacks with other bonuses, making your subsequent training much faster.

### Quest 2: The Coliseum Challenge
From the first island, travel straight, take a left, and head toward the Coliseum area. The NPC is tucked away near the edge of the zone, away from the main combat arena.

| Objective | Requirement | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Haki Rolls** | Roll Haki 1,000 times | Increased Critical Rate |
| **Kaido Kills** | Defeat Kaido 5,000 times | Massive EXP Drop |

While 5,000 kills may seem daunting, this is best completed by setting up an AFK macro overnight. Kaido's spawn rate has been adjusted in 2026 to facilitate this high-volume requirement.

## Advanced Progression: The Secret Boss Grinds

As you move into the mid-to-late game, the **World Fighters simulator** quests become significantly more difficult, often requiring you to interact with "Secret" tier units and bosses.

### Quest 3: The Broly Unit Hunt
This quest is located deep within the map, situated next to the secret boss area. You must travel to the very back of the zone to find the NPC hidden in a lower trench.

1.  **Open Units:** You must open 1,000 units at the gacha altars.
2.  **Secret Pulls:** You must successfully pull the **Broly Unit** 4 times.

> **Warning:** Pulling Broly is based on a low percentage chance. It is highly recommended to use "Luck Potions" and ensure you have the "Fast Open" gamepass enabled to speed up this process.

### Quest 4: Tempest Nation Mastery
The final major side quest is found in Zone 6, known as Tempest Nation. Navigate behind the largest building in the zone to find the NPC. This quest is designed for players who have already achieved a high power level.

| Requirement | Target | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kill Guy** | 5,000 Times | Guy is the Zone 6 Secret Boss |
| **Tempest Invasion** | Reach Wave 25 | Requires high AOE damage |

Completing the Tempest Nation quest provides one of the strongest endgame buffs currently available, which is essential for surviving the higher waves of the Invasion mode.

## Efficiency Strategies for 2026

To maximize your time in **World Fighters simulator**, you should overlap your activities whenever possible. Many players make the mistake of focusing on one quest at a time. Instead, try the following:

*   **Multi-Tasking:** If you are grinding for the 5,000 Kaido kills, make sure you are also spending your earned currency on unit rolls to progress the "Open 1,000 Units" objective simultaneously.
*   **Team Buffs:** Join a squad or a private server with friends. High-level bosses like Kaido and Guy die much faster with multiple players, and in most cases, kill credit is shared among all contributors.
*   **Zone Prioritization:** Do not rush to Zone 6 if you haven't completed the 1.5x power multiplier from Quest 1. The difficulty spike in Tempest Nation is brutal without that foundational buff.

For more detailed stats on unit tiers, you can visit the [Official Roblox Group](https://www.roblox.com/groups/12345678/World-Fighters-Official) to see the latest patch notes and community discussions regarding secret unit drop rates.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to AFK for the 5,000 Kaido kills?**

A: The best spot is directly at the Kaido spawn point in the Coliseum area. Ensure your "Auto-Clicker" is set to a 0.5-second interval and that you have enough inventory space for any drops, though the 2026 QOL update mostly handles rewards automatically.

**Q: Are the side quest rewards in World Fighters simulator permanent?**

A: Yes, the power multipliers and stat buffs gained from these specific NPC side quests are permanent and stay with your character even after you prestige or move to new zones.

**Q: I found the NPC but I can't accept the quest. Why?**

A: Some quests have prerequisite levels or require you to have unlocked the specific zone first. Ensure you have reached the necessary island and completed any main story missions associated with that area.

**Q: Is the Broly unit really required 4 times for Quest 3?**

A: Yes, and because Broly is a secret-tier unit, this is often considered the hardest side quest in the game. Most players save their "Super Luck" boosts specifically for this objective.
