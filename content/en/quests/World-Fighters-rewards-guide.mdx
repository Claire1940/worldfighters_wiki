## Daily and Time-Based Reward Systems

One of the most reliable ways to progress as a free-to-play player is through the passive reward systems. Upon entering the game, you will notice various reward icons that fill up based on your active playtime. These are categorized into Daily Rewards and Time Rewards.

While the Daily Rewards provide a steady stream of currency and basic items, the Time Rewards are where players can find significant boosts to their "Crystal Luck" and "Drop Rates." On release in 2026, these rewards start small but scale significantly the longer you stay in a single session.

| Reward Type | Primary Contents | Frequency | Importance |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily Login** | Gems, Potion Packs, Coins | Once every 24 hours | High |
| **Time Rewards** | Crystals, Luck Boosts, Star Opens | Every 5 to 60 minutes | **Critical** |
| **Follow Rewards** | Global Damage & Energy Boosts | One-time per account | Essential |
| **Chest Rewards** | Map-specific currency, Keys | Spawns periodically | Medium |

> **💡 Tip:** Always claim your Follow Rewards immediately after starting a new save. The permanent damage boosts are calculated multiplicatively with your equipped units, providing a massive early-game advantage.

## Mastering Trials and Forging Passives

To truly excel, you must move beyond basic NPC farming and enter the Trials. World Fighters features a tiered Trial system: Easy, Medium, and Hard. These are not just for testing your damage; they are the primary source of materials needed to forge passives for your units.

Trials function as a wave-based defense mode. Reaching Wave 50 on the Hard difficulty is considered the pinnacle of achievement in 2026, granting the "Insane" feat which significantly boosts your Gacha roll luck.

| Trial Difficulty | Key Rewards | Recommended Power |
|---|---|---|
| **Easy** | Trial Shards, Wood Katana, Diamonds | 100K - 1M |
| **Medium** | Passive Shards, Fruit Tokens | 50M - 500M |
| **Hard** | **Dark Blade**, Legendary Shards, Soul Stars | 10B+ |

The items dropped in trials, such as the Wood Katana or Trial Shards, are used at the Forge. Forging allows you to roll for passives on your units, which can grant bonuses to attack range, critical multiplier, and even "Shiny Chance."

The Haki system works in tandem with your accessories. By spending crystals, you can roll for different Haki tiers that provide a flat multiplier to all damage dealt.

## Map Progression and Secret Units

World Fighters Simulator launched with five distinct maps: Dress Rosa, Marine Fortress, Dragon Verse, Slime Verse, and a final endgame zone. Each map contains a unique "Secret" unit that can only be obtained through low-probability gacha rolls or specific "Wanted Poster" quests.

For example, in the Dragon Verse map, players can hunt for the **Broly** and **Diablo** secret units. These units possess base power levels that dwarf even the strongest mythics from previous worlds.

1.  **Dress Rosa:** Focus on unlocking the Luffy Gear 5 secret.
2.  **Marine Fortress:** Farm for the "Rubber Fruit" through the Wanted Poster system.
3.  **Dragon Verse:** Collect Dragon Balls to use on the **Wish Roulette** for race tokens.
4.  **Slime Verse:** Maximize your "Anti-AFK" settings to farm Slime Keys for the skill tree.

The Skill Tree system is another layer of rewards. You will need approximately **90,000 Keys** to fully max out the tree in 2026. This investment yields a permanent 3x power boost and increased attack range, which is vital for clearing NPCs in groups.

## The Awakening and Rank-Up System

In many simulators, ranking up resets all your progress. In World Fighters, the "Awakening" system is more forgiving. While it resets your current power to a baseline, it preserves your rank and grants a permanent multiplier to all future power gains.

You can initiate your first Awakening once you reach 10,000 Power. Each subsequent Awakening requires a higher power threshold but offers diminishing returns unless paired with high-tier "Aura" upgrades. Auras, such as the Flame III Aura, provide up to a 6.5x power multiplier and can be rolled using crystals in the Dragon Verse map.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the [Official Roblox Group](https://www.roblox.com/groups/1) to stay updated on community codes, which often provide free Awakening shards and Luck potions during weekend events.

## Achievement Feats and Permanent Boosts

The "Feats" menu is a checklist of your accomplishments. Unlike time-based rewards, these are permanent milestones that provide account-wide buffs.

*   **Time Played:** Increases your passive Crystal income.
*   **Store Opens:** Improves "Star Open Speed" (essential for mass-hatching units).
*   **Defeated Enemies:** Boosts your base Drop Rate.
*   **Trial Completions:** Increases the quality of shards found in chests.

By focusing on these feats early, you reduce the "grind" associated with later maps. The "Claim All" feature in the Feats menu allows you to quickly grab rewards as you hit milestones like 10,000 enemies defeated or 50 trials cleared.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to use the World Fighters rewards guide for fast leveling?**

A: Focus on completing the "Main Quests" first to unlock new maps. Use your Crystals only during "Luck Events" and prioritize upgrading your "Attack Range" in the Trial Upgrade menu to clear NPCs faster.

**Q: How do I get the Secret Angel Race?**

A: The Angel Race is a rare drop from the Dragon Wish Roulette. You must collect seven Dragon Balls through map exploration and NPC drops to earn a spin on the wheel.

**Q: Does the "Remote Access" game pass include all stores?**

A: The Remote Access game pass specifically allows you to access the Unit Star Store from anywhere on the map. It does not necessarily grant remote access to the Forge or the Dragon Merchant, which still require you to visit their specific NPCs.

**Q: Are Shiny units worth the effort in World Fighters?**

A: Yes. A Shiny Secret unit like Broly has approximately 1.5x the power of a standard Secret unit. In the 2026 endgame, having a full team of Shiny units is often the requirement for clearing Wave 50 of the Hard Trials.
