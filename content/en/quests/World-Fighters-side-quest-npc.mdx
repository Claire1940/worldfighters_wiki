## Finding the First World Fighters side quest npc

The first **World Fighters side quest npc** you should seek out is located near the beginning of the game, but it is tucked away in a spot that many new players overlook. To find them, head to the first island and look for a small interior area or building near the main hub.

Once inside, you will encounter the NPC who tasks you with a relatively simple grind. You need to defeat enemies 750 times. While this might seem like a lot for a beginner, the 1.5x power reward is monumental for early-game progression.

> **💡 Tip:** Use your fastest-attacking units for this quest. Since the goal is the number of kills rather than the strength of the enemy, farming lower-level mobs is the most efficient way to clear this quickly.

## The Coliseum Side Quest: Kaido and Haki

As you progress further, you will encounter the Coliseum area. This is where the difficulty spikes significantly. To find the next **World Fighters side quest npc**, follow the main path from the first island, take a sharp left, and head toward the outskirts of the Coliseum.

This NPC offers one of the most notorious grinds in the game. You are required to use Ryu Haki 1,000 times and defeat the boss Kaido 5,000 times.

| Requirement | Quantity | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ryu Haki Usage** | 1,000 | Set a macro or use auto-clicker |
| **Kaido Kills** | 5,000 | Best done in a private server to avoid kill-stealing |

Most players recommend an overnight AFK session for the Kaido kills. Given Kaido's high health pool, ensure your team is optimized for DPS before attempting this. The community has often requested a nerf to this specific quest, but as of 2026, the 5,000-kill requirement remains the standard for those seeking the top-tier rewards.

## Navigating to the Secret Boss Side Quest NPC

The third major NPC is located deep within the Secret Boss zone. To find them, you need to head toward the back of the map, past the standard boss spawns, toward the hidden area where the secret bosses reside. The NPC is located down a small path, making them very easy to miss if you aren't looking specifically for the quest icon.

This quest is unique because it involves the gacha mechanics of the game. You are required to:
1. Open 1,000 capsules/units.
2. Successfully pull the Broly unit 4 times.

Broly is considered a "Secret" or "Legendary" tier unit, meaning the drop rate is incredibly low. This quest is designed for long-term players who have accumulated a significant amount of currency.

> **Warning:** Do not attempt this quest until you have a stable source of income. Opening 1,000 units without a high "Luck" stat can result in a massive waste of resources without obtaining the necessary Broly units.

## Endgame Progression: Zone 6 (Tempest Nation)

The final **World Fighters side quest npc** currently in the game is found in Zone 6, also known as the Tempest Nation. This area is inspired by high-level fantasy anime and features some of the toughest mobs in the game.

To find the NPC, go straight through the main entrance of Zone 6, then wrap around the large central building. The NPC is standing behind the structure, away from the main combat zones.

### Quest Objectives for Tempest Nation:
*   **Kill Might Guy 5,000 times:** Might Guy is the secret boss of this zone. He has high speed and high damage, so a defensive pet or unit is recommended.
*   **Reach Wave 25 in Tempest Invasion:** This is a tower-defense style mini-game within the zone. Reaching Wave 25 requires a coordinated team or a very high-level solo build.

| Objective | Difficulty | Recommended Power Level |

Completing this quest provides an endgame buff that is essential for competing in the global leaderboards. Most top-tier players prioritize this quest as soon as they unlock Zone 6 to maximize their efficiency in the Tempest Invasion mode.

## Optimization Strategies for Quest Completion

To successfully complete all quests from every **World Fighters side quest npc**, you need to manage your time and resources effectively. Here are the best strategies for 2026:

1.  **Multi-Questing:** Always check if a side quest objective overlaps with a main quest or a daily challenge. If you have to kill a certain number of enemies, try to do it while farming for a specific drop to kill two birds with one stone.
2.  **Private Servers:** For boss-kill quests (like Kaido or Guy), private servers are highly recommended. This prevents other players from slowing down your kill-per-minute rate.
3.  **Luck Buffs:** Before attempting the Broly quest, ensure you have every possible luck buff active. This includes potions, equipment, and any temporary world events that increase secret unit drop rates.
4.  **Auto-Clickers:** For the "Usage" quests (like Haki), a simple auto-clicker set to the skill's cooldown will allow you to complete the requirement while you are away from your computer.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the World Fighters side quest npc for the 1.5x power multiplier?**

A: This NPC is located on the first island. Look for a building near the spawn area; the NPC is inside and requires you to defeat 750 enemies to unlock the permanent power boost.

**Q: Do side quest rewards stack?**

A: Yes, the rewards from different side quest NPCs stack multiplicatively with your base stats. This is why completing them is vital for reaching the "Trillions" and "Quadrillions" of power levels required for endgame content.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get the Broly unit for the secret quest?**

A: The fastest way is to maximize your Luck stat through potions and equipment, then use the "Fast Open" or "Auto Open" feature on the specific unit capsule. It is purely RNG-based, so be prepared for a long grind.

**Q: Can I complete the Kaido quest in a group?**

A: While you can fight Kaido in a group, you must ensure that you are dealing enough damage to receive credit for the kill. In World Fighters Simulator, kill credit is generally given to the player or team that deals the most significant portion of damage, but private servers are still the safest bet for guaranteed progression.
