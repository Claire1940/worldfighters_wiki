## Quest 1: The Starter Zone Power Boost

The first of the **World Fighters side quests** is located right near the beginning of your journey. This quest is often overlooked by players rushing to the second island, but it provides one of the most essential early-game buffs.

### Location and Navigation
To find this NPC, look for a building near the initial spawn area. You will need to head inside the structure to locate the quest giver. The interior is relatively small, so the NPC should be immediately visible once you enter.

### Objectives and Rewards
The requirements for this quest are straightforward but require a bit of time:
*   **Objective:** Defeat the local mobs 750 times.
*   **Reward:** A permanent **1.5x Power Multiplier**.

> **💡 Tip:** Use a fast-attacking unit to clear these 750 kills quickly. Since the mobs are low-level, speed is more important than raw damage output.

## Quest 2: The Coliseum Challenge (Kaido & Haki)

As you progress to the first major island, you will encounter the Coliseum area. This is where the difficulty begins to ramp up significantly, requiring players to engage in long-term farming.

### Location and Navigation
From the first island's main hub:
1.  Go straight from the spawn point.
2.  Take a sharp left after the first set of buildings.
3.  Continue straight until you reach the Coliseum perimeter.
4.  The NPC is tucked away near the edge of the Coliseum area.

### Objectives and Rewards
This quest is notorious for its high kill count, which many players prefer to complete via an auto-clicker or AFK script overnight.
*   **Objective 1:** Use "Rule Haki" 1,000 times.
*   **Objective 2:** Kill Kaido 5,000 times.

| Target | Count | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Haki Usage** | 1,000 | Set your ability to auto-cast while farming mobs. |
| **Kaido** | 5,000 | Farm the Coliseum boss spawn point for maximum efficiency. |

## Quest 3: The Secret Boss & Broly Unit Hunt

The third quest in the series focuses on the luck-based mechanics of the game, specifically the unit summoning system. This quest is located near the secret boss area, which is usually found toward the back of the island.

### Location and Navigation
To find this NPC, navigate toward the secret boss zone. Instead of entering the boss arena, look for a path that leads downward. The NPC is located in a lower-tier area, making it somewhat hidden if you are only looking at eye level.

### Objectives and Rewards
This quest tests your patience with the gacha system and your ability to farm currency.
*   **Objective 1:** Open 1,000 Units (Eggs).
*   **Objective 2:** Obtain the **Broly Unit** 4 times.

> **Warning:** Broly is considered a rare/secret-tier unit. Ensure you have enough inventory space and currency before starting this quest, as 1,000 opens can fill your storage quickly.

## Quest 4: Tempest Nation (Zone 6)

The final major side quest currently available in 2026 is located in Zone 6, also known as the Tempest Nation. This quest is designed for endgame players and offers rewards that are essential for tackling the highest-level content.

### Location and Navigation
Once you reach Zone 6, head toward the large central building. You will need to go behind this building to find the NPC. The area is quite large, so keep an eye out for the quest icon behind the architectural structures.

### Objectives and Rewards
This quest combines boss farming with the "Invasion" game mode, which is a wave-based survival challenge.
*   **Objective 1:** Kill the "Guy" character 5,000 times.
*   **Objective 2:** Reach Wave 25 in the Tempest Invasion.

The "Guy" character is often found in the secret boss area of this zone. Reaching Wave 25 in the Tempest Invasion requires a balanced team of high-DPS units and at least one support unit for healing or buffs.

| Requirement | Type | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Guy Kills** | Boss Farming | Single-target DPS |
| **Wave 25** | Survival | AoE Damage & Sustain |

## Strategies for Efficient Quest Completion

Completing all the **World Fighters side quests** can be a daunting task if you try to do it all manually. To optimize your progress in 2026, consider the following strategies:

1.  **Utilize Auto-Claim:** Always ensure the "Auto-Claim" toggle is ON in your quest menu. This prevents you from wasting time traveling back to NPCs.
2.  **AFK Farming:** For the 5,000 kill requirements (Kaido and Guy), set your character to farm at the boss spawn point overnight. Ensure your units are strong enough to one-shot or two-shot the boss to maximize kills per hour.
3.  **Multi-Tasking:** You can often progress multiple objectives at once. For example, while farming Kaido for Quest 2, you should also be using your Haki abilities to satisfy the usage requirement.
4.  **Inventory Management:** When attempting Quest 3 (opening 1,000 units), use the "Auto-Sell" feature for lower-rarity units so your inventory doesn't clog up before you find the 4 Broly units.

For more detailed stats on units and boss health, you can check the [official World Fighters Simulator community pages](https://www.roblox.com/games/14251433602/World-Fighters-Simulator) to stay updated on balance changes.

## FAQ

**Q: Do side quest rewards stack with other multipliers?**

A: Yes, rewards like the 1.5x power multiplier from the first quest are permanent and stack multiplicatively with other game passes and buffs you might have active.

**Q: Is the Broly unit required for Quest 3 a secret rarity?**

A: Broly is a high-rarity unit, often classified as a secret or legendary depending on the current update. Obtaining four of them requires a significant amount of luck and currency.

**Q: Where can I find the "Guy" boss for the Zone 6 quest?**

A: Guy is located in the secret boss area within Zone 6 (Tempest Nation). He has a specific spawn timer, so it is best to farm him in a server where you can consistently hit the spawn point.

**Q: Can I complete World Fighters side quests in any order?**

A: While you can find the NPCs in any order, it is highly recommended to complete them sequentially. The power boosts from the earlier quests make the 5,000-kill grinds in the later zones much faster.
