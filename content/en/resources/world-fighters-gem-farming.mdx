## Maximizing Power with Divine Energy

One of the most overlooked aspects of **world fighters gem farming** involves the use of Diamonds to secure the Divine Energy passive. This passive is a game-changer, offering a massive 1.55x power multiplier to your main character. Many players waste their gems on low-tier rolls, but the guaranteed method for Divine Energy is far more efficient.

To obtain Divine Energy with a 100% success rate, follow this recipe in the upgrade menu:
1. Collect **10 Wooden Katanas** (easily found in the early worlds).
2. Accumulate **6 Diamonds**.
3. Combine them in the Passive menu for a guaranteed Divine Energy roll.

> **💡 Tip:** Never gamble on low-percentage passive rolls. Always save your gems and diamonds until you can guarantee a 100% success rate for high-tier buffs like Divine Energy.

## Essential Equipment for Gem and Power Optimization

Your loadout dictates how effectively you can farm. From Haki to Devil Fruits, every slot in your inventory should be dedicated to either increasing your raw power or boosting your luck for better drops.

### Haki and Devil Fruits
Haki is essential for power gain. While the basic tiers are easy to obtain, you should aim for the Secret or Mythical Haki as soon as possible. These tiers provide exponential growth to your power gains, making every click worth significantly more. Similarly, Devil Fruits provide massive damage and power boosts. The Secret Devil Fruit, for instance, can grant up to 4x power and 5x damage, which drastically reduces the time spent on boss kills.

### The Sword Meta: Dual Wielding

By equipping two high-tier swords like Yoru, you can achieve a 10x power multiplier. This makes **world fighters gem farming** much easier because you can clear mobs and bosses in a fraction of the time, leading to faster chest drops and crystal accumulation.

## Advanced Farming: Wanted Posters and Accessories

The "Wanted" system in World Fighters Simulator provides a structured path for progression. Each world features specific posters that grant characters with unique buffs. For those focused on **world fighters gem farming**, obtaining characters like Ace (One Piece world) or Diablo (Slime world) is essential. These characters act as pillars for your team, providing the necessary stats to farm the "New World" events where secret accessories drop frequently.

### Accessory Farming Locations
Accessories are the "carries" of the late game. They buff everything from damage to movement speed and drop rates. Every secret boss in the game has a chance to drop an accessory for various slots:
*   **Back:** Capes and wings for mobility.
*   **Body:** Armor for damage reduction and health.
*   **Head/Mask:** Critical hit chance and luck modifiers.
*   **Waist:** Additional stat multipliers.

The most efficient way to farm these is to enter the latest event game modes added in the 2026 updates. These modes have higher drop rates for secret accessories, often allowing a dedicated player to fully kit out their character within just a few hours of focused grinding.

## AFK Strategies for Consistent Gains

Not everyone has the time to actively click for hours. This is where AFK (Away From Keyboard) strategies become vital for **world fighters gem farming**. The "Remote Stars" game pass is the single best investment for free-to-play or low-spending players. It allows you to automate kills, collect drops, and even summon new characters while you are away.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When AFK farming, ensure your "Auto-Sell" or "Auto-Delete" settings are correctly configured for your inventory. You don't want to miss out on a Secret drop because your inventory was full of common items!

### The Renaming Trick
A secret trick used by pro players to squeeze out extra power is the renaming mechanic. By renaming your characters (such as Naruto) and "spamming W's" or other characters, you can trigger a stat refresh. Aim for at least a 1.6x power roll on your main units. While the max is approximately 1.75x, getting close to that number will make a noticeable difference in your farming efficiency.

## Completing the Index for Permanent Buffs
Every map in World Fighters Simulator has an Index. Collecting every character and item in a specific world grants permanent damage and power bonuses. Even if you have moved on to a harder world, it is often worth returning to earlier maps to finish your Index. These bonuses are additive and provide a massive foundation for your overall stats.

1. **Check your Index daily** to see which common or rare units you are missing.
2. **Use low-tier stars** to fill out the common slots without wasting your high-tier gems.
3. **Unlock inventory slots** to ensure you can hold enough units to complete the collection.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to start world fighters gem farming as a beginner?**

A: Focus on the Time Trial immediately. Even if you can only clear the first few rooms, the crystals you earn there are far more plentiful than what you find in the starting world. Use those crystals to upgrade your Attack Speed first.

**Q: Are the 2x Power game passes worth it?**

A: While they are helpful, the **2x Sword** pass is generally considered better value in 2026. Because you can equip two powerful swords, the multiplicative effect on your power gain far exceeds the flat 2x bonus from the power pass.

**Q: How do I get more Diamonds for the Divine Energy passive?**

A: Diamonds are primarily earned through daily quests, chest rewards in higher worlds, and as rare drops from secret bosses. Consistent **world fighters gem farming** in the latest unlocked map is the most reliable way to accumulate them.

**Q: Why isn't my power increasing even though I'm clicking?**

A: Check your Awakening level. If you haven't started the Awakening process, your power per click will hit a ceiling very quickly. Turn on "Auto-Awaken" and ensure you have enough crystals to sustain the process.
