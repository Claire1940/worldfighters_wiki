## Step-by-Step Conversion: From MBX to World Fighters Keys

The process of getting your keys involves several layers of token conversion. This "Warp and Swap" method ensures that the tokens are compatible with the game's specific exchange station. Follow these steps carefully to avoid errors.

### Step 1: Warping MBX to MBXL
Once you have funded your MARLEX wallet with MBX, you must convert them to MBXL. This is known as the "Warp" process. Within the wallet interface, locate the Warp section and select your MBX tokens. Confirm the transaction to receive the XL version of the currency.

### Step 2: Swapping MBXL to GMBXL
The exchange station specifically requires **GMBXL** to purchase the World Fighting Championship keys. 
1.  Navigate to the swap station within your MARLEX interface.
2.  Select **MBXL** as your source and **GMBXL** as your destination.
3.  Agree to the terms and confirm the transaction by entering your trading password.
4.  Wait for the "Transaction Success" notification before proceeding.

### Step 3: The Final Exchange for PGK
With GMBXL in your wallet, you can now head to the Pocket Girls Exchange Station.
1.  Connect your MARLEX wallet to the station.
2.  Log in using your registered game email.
3.  Select the option to exchange **GMBXL to PGK** (World Fighting Championship Keys).
4.  Input the amount of keys you wish to purchase.
5.  Click the exchange button and confirm the transaction.

> **💡 Tip:** The final exchange from GMBXL to PGK typically does not incur gas fees, making it a "free" transaction in terms of network costs, though you are still spending the underlying tokens.

## Entering the Arena: Using Your World Fighters Keys Guide

Once the keys are in your account, they will appear in your in-game interface. To use them, look for the "World Fighting Championship" icon, usually located on the top left of the main screen.

When you enter the arena, you are placed in a combat scenario against a boss monster. Your goal is to survive as many rounds as possible and deal maximum damage. Even if your characters are currently "weak" or low-level, it is beneficial to enter the arena to establish a baseline ranking.

| Feature | Description |

### Performance Tiers
The game categorizes players based on their performance against the monster. If you die early (for example, at Monster Level 8), you may find yourself in the "D" rank. However, as you upgrade your Pocket Girls, you will see your score improve. The higher your rank, the better the rewards you can claim for the Daily Shop.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I see my ranking immediately after using a key?**

A: The World Fighting Championship ranking system does not update in real-time. It typically takes about 10 minutes for the server to process your combat data and place you in the global standings. Check back after a short wait to see your updated info.

**Q: Do World Fighters keys expire if I don't use them?**

A: While the keys themselves stay in your inventory until used, the "season" or ranking period resets every Monday. This **World Fighters keys guide** recommends using your keys early in the week to secure a spot on the leaderboard, then potentially using more later in the week if you have significantly upgraded your characters.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many keys I can buy?**

A: The limit is generally determined by your available GMBXL balance. However, keep in mind that you only need a certain number of entries to maintain or improve a rank. Over-purchasing keys without having the character strength to back them up may not be the most efficient use of your tokens.

**Q: What happens if I die early in the arena?**

A: Even if you die early, your score is recorded. In the early stages of the game, it is common to rank in the "D" tier. As you level up your girls and improve your gear, you will naturally progress to higher tiers. Every entry provides some value, so don't be discouraged by an early exit.
