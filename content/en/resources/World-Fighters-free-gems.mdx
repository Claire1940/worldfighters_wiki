## Smart Spending: Convert Gems into Real Power

Getting **World Fighters free gems** is only half of the process. Spending incorrectly can delay your progress by days.

A common mistake is buying low-impact options before securing progression multipliers. If your goal is faster power scaling, use a performance-first purchase order.

### Recommended gamepass purchase order (gem path)

| Order | Gamepass Type | Typical Impact | Recommended Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Double Power** | Accelerates base stat growth | As soon as affordable |
| **2** | **Double Damage** | Faster enemy clears | Right after Double Power |
| **3** | **Dual Swords** | Better combat throughput | Mid-early progression |

Why this order works:
- Power scaling improves everything else.
- Damage scaling reduces time per kill.
- Combat utility upgrades become more valuable once your base multipliers are active.

> **Warning:** If a shop option lets you buy gems with Robux, compare its value to direct gamepass purchase first. In many cases, direct pass purchase gives better progression per Robux spent.

### When to hold gems instead of spending

Hold your gems when:
- You are close to a major multiplier pass.
- A patch is likely to add stronger gem sink options.
- You are missing only a small amount for a high-value unlock.

Spend immediately when:
- You can unlock a multiplier that boosts all future farming.
- You are stuck on progression gates where damage/power boost solves clear speed issues.
