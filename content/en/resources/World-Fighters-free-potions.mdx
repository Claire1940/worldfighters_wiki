## FAQ

**Q: Do World Fighters free potions expire if I don't use them?**

A: No, potions stored in your inventory do not expire. However, once you consume a potion, the timer will continue to count down even if you log out of the game in most versions of the 2026 build.

**Q: Can I trade potions with other players?**

A: Currently, potions are "Soulbound" to your account to prevent market inflation. You cannot trade **World Fighters free potions** directly, but you can help friends by using "Area Buff" items if they are available during special events.

**Q: What is the rarest potion in the game?**

A: The "Ultimate Ascension Potion" is currently the rarest. It provides a massive boost to all stats for 60 minutes and can typically only be obtained through extremely rare event drops or completing the highest tier of the Battle Pass.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many potions I can hold?**

A: Most players have a base limit of 99 per potion type. You can increase this storage capacity by upgrading your "Alchemist Satchel" in the main city using gold earned from quests.
