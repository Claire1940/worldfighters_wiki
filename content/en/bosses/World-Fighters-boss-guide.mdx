## Detailed Map Navigation

### The Coliseum: Finding Kaido
The Coliseum is one of the earliest maps where you can encounter a secret boss. Finding Kaido is relatively straightforward compared to later worlds. Once you spawn into the Coliseum, ignore the central fighting pit and head toward the outer perimeter. Follow the curved wall until you reach the rear section of the arena. Kaido spawns in an open area that is often overlooked by players rushing to the next world. The **Kaido Cape** (also referred to as the Admiral Cape) is a legendary tier item that provides a solid boost to your base damage.

### Marineford: The Ace Encounter
Marineford is a sprawling map, but the secret boss location is surprisingly simple if you know where to look. From the teleport spawn point, walk directly straight toward the water. Instead of heading toward the main fortress, veer into the far corner of the map. You will find Ace waiting there.

The developers recently buffed the AoE (Area of Effect) circles for upgrades in this area, making it easier to manage your stats while you wait for the boss to respawn. The **Ace Hat** is a highly coveted legendary accessory that is essential for mid-game builds in 2026.

### Capsule Corp: Goku Black
The first Dragon Ball-inspired map, Capsule Corp, features Goku Black as its secret resident. This is perhaps the easiest boss to locate in the entire game. Upon entering the map, simply walk straight past the main Capsule Corp building. He is positioned in the open back lot. Defeating him gives you the chance to earn the **Saiyan Tail**, an accessory that provides a significant multiplier to your power gain.

## The Most Difficult Secret Bosses

While the early bosses are easy to find, the later maps introduce environmental puzzles and hidden entrances that can frustrate even veteran players.

### The Tournament Map Underground
The Tournament of Power-inspired map contains a boss that many players miss entirely. To find this secret warrior, follow these steps:
1. Locate the central fountain near the map entrance.
2. Head straight past the fountain toward the edge of the map.
3. Look for a cluster of trees in the far corner.
4. Navigate to the very last tree in the corner; there is a hidden hole in the ground.
5. Drop into the hole to find the underground boss room.

This boss drops the **Mythical Chestplate**, which is currently one of the best defensive items in World Fighters. Because this location is so well-hidden, it is often the best place to farm without interference from other players.

### Slime World: Rimuru's Hideout
The Slime World map (based on *That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime*) features a secret boss that requires a bit of patience to find. You need to navigate through the dense forest area until you find a small alcove hidden behind the foliage. This boss drops the **Rimuru Mask**, a Mythical accessory that is considered "Best in Slot" for many endgame speed-running builds.

## AFK Farming and Efficiency

To maximize your gains from this **World Fighters boss guide**, you should utilize AFK (Away From Keyboard) farming techniques. Since secret bosses have a set respawn timer, standing on their spawn point with an auto-clicker or the in-game auto-fight feature is the most effective way to obtain Mythical drops.

1. **Positioning:** Stand directly on the boss spawn point to ensure your character triggers the fight immediately upon respawn.
2. **AoE Upgrades:** Ensure you have invested in the AoE range upgrades. This allows your character to hit the boss even if it moves slightly or if your character is pushed back during the fight.
3. **Inventory Management:** Before going AFK, clear out your inventory of common items so you have plenty of space for the Legendary and Mythical drops.

> **Warning:** While AFK farming is a common practice, ensure you are following the [official Roblox community guidelines](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/203313410-Roblox-Community-Standards) regarding third-party software. Always use the built-in game features whenever possible.
