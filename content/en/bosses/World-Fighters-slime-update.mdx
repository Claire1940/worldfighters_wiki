## Exploring the Jura Forest and Slime Verse

The core of the **World Fighters slime update** is the introduction of Zone 5, known as the Jura Forest. This area is teeming with familiar foes from the anime, ranging from lowly goblins to high-tier leaders. Progression through this zone requires a significant jump in energy and power compared to previous areas, making it a true test for mid-game players.

To unlock Jura Forest, players must first defeat the bosses in the Dragon Arena, specifically Shiny Guru Ginyu. Once inside, the primary objective is to farm materials like "Slime Power" and "Dragon Tokens," which are necessary for the new upgrade systems.

## The Accessory System and Avatar Progression

Accessories can be obtained through various methods, including boss drops, time trials, and the new "Oatmeal" shop in the Tempest Federation.

## Mastering the Tempest Federation Upgrades

Zone 6, the Tempest Federation, serves as the endgame hub for the **World Fighters slime update**. Here, players can spend their hard-earned Dragon Tokens and Slime Power on permanent upgrades that affect their entire account.

The upgrade terminal in Tempest Federation offers several paths:
1.  **Attack Speed:** Increases the rate at which your units click/attack.
2.  **Gotcha Luck:** Improves the odds of pulling Mythic and Secret units from stars.
3.  **Damage Multipliers:** Flat increases to your total output.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Attack Speed upgrade provides diminishing returns (starting at 2% per level). Focus on Gotcha Luck first if you are still hunting for a full Mythic team.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Jura Forest in the World Fighters slime update?**

A: You must progress through the Dragon Arena and defeat the final bosses there. Once you have sufficient energy and have cleared the prerequisites, the portal to Jura Forest will become accessible in the map menu.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Mythic Rimuru?**

A: The most efficient method is to equip a full luck team (units with luck passives), consume a Luck Potion II, and use the "Fast Open" game pass if available. Focus on opening stars in the Jura Forest specifically, as the pity system will eventually guarantee a Mythic pull.

**Q: Do accessories stay equipped if I change my units?**

A: Yes, accessories are bound to your avatar, not your units. This means the buffs from your head, back, and body slots remain active regardless of which fighters you have equipped in your active party.

**Q: Is the Goku Black avatar worth the 2,500 kill grind?**

A: Absolutely. While it takes several hours of AFK grinding, the 11x buff at level 25 is currently the second-best avatar multiplier in the game, making it vital for high-wave Dragon Defense and leaderboard pushes.
