## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way for World Fighters how to get Kaido?**

A: The fastest method is to farm the Dragon Defense mode on 2x speed. This generates the highest amount of tokens per hour. Save these tokens and only spend them when you have a Luck Potion or a Luck Event active.

**Q: Can I get Kaido through trading?**

A: Trading mechanics vary by server version, but generally, Secret units like Kaido are "Soulbound" or require high-level trading tokens. It is usually more reliable to grind the tokens and summon him yourself.

**Q: Does the 3x speed modifier increase drop rates?**

A: No, the speed modifier only increases how fast enemies move and how quickly waves progress. It does not directly change the RNG percentage for unit drops, but it allows you to complete more runs in a shorter amount of time.

**Q: Which world has the Kaido banner?**

A: Kaido is typically found in the "Dragon Arena" or "Wano" inspired world. Look for the Star that features a dragon-themed silhouette or the "Secret" label on its drop list.
