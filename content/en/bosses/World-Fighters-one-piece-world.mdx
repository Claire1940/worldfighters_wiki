## Mastering the Unit Gacha and Avatars

In the **World Fighters One Piece world**, your units are your lifeline. As a free-to-play player, you can initially equip three units. These units provide power-per-click bonuses. For instance, a basic Mr. Buggy might offer a small boost, but landing a higher-rarity unit like Shanks significantly accelerates your power accumulation.

Units can also be "Shiny," which are rare variants with vastly superior stats. While grinding the base areas, keep an eye out for Shiny King or Shiny Robin Luco spawns. Defeating these specific enemies can sometimes yield better drops or progress toward unique crafting recipes.

### Avatar Leveling
Unlike standard units, your Avatar (such as Mr. Fire Face) levels up as you defeat enemies. This provides a permanent stat increase that persists even if you swap out your secondary units.

## The Haki and Passive Systems

To truly dominate the **World Fighters One Piece world**, you must engage with the Haki system. Haki is unlocked using "Keys," which have a 50% drop rate from certain bosses like Mr. Buggy. Once you have enough keys, you can roll for different Haki types that provide global multipliers to your damage and power.

The Purple Amethyst Haki is a standout in the early game, offering a 2x Power multiplier and a 0.25x Damage boost. While the damage boost seems small, the power multiplier allows you to reach the requirements for "Awakening" much faster.

### Time Trials and Shards
The Time Trial mode is accessible via the "Modes" menu. Completing these trials rewards you with Shards, which are used to forge Passives for your units. Passives add unique traits such as increased attack distance (range) or faster click speeds.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend all your Shards on low-tier unit passives. Save them for Epic or Legendary units, as the crafting chance for high-tier passives can be quite low.

## Advanced Progression: Awakening and Marine Fortress

Once you have conquered the initial islands of the **World Fighters One Piece world**, you will move toward the Marine Fortress. This area introduces the "Fruit" gacha and the Evolution Skill Tree.

Awakening is a critical milestone. The first awakening typically costs 5,000 power, with the price scaling significantly for subsequent tiers (e.g., 45,000 for the second tier). Awakening resets some progress but grants a permanent, massive multiplier to your overall strength.

### Crafting Requirements
High-tier units often require specific crafting materials found only in the One Piece zones:
1. **Rare Usopp:** Requires killing 250 King enemies and obtaining 2 Rubber Fruits.
2. **Rubber Fruit:** A rare drop (approximately 1% chance) from high-level Marine Fortress enemies.
3. **Pirate Skulls:** Used in the passive index to guarantee specific craft chances.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more Rubber Fruits in the World Fighters One Piece world?**

A: Rubber Fruits are rare drops with a 1% chance from enemies in the Marine Fortress area. It is recommended to use a Luck Potion before farming these enemies to increase your drop rate.

**Q: What is the best Haki for beginners?**

A: The Purple Amethyst Haki is the best entry-level Haki because it doubles your power gain, allowing you to hit Awakening milestones much faster than with standard damage-focused Haki.

**Q: How does the "Auto-Join" feature work in Time Trials?**

A: To use Auto-Join, you must first reach Trial Level 5. Once unlocked, the game will automatically enter you into the next available trial, allowing you to farm Shards for passives while you are semi-AFK.

**Q: Can I take my One Piece units to the Dragon Ball world?**

A: Yes, units obtained in the One Piece world can be used in subsequent worlds like the Dragon Ball or Slime areas, though you will eventually want to replace them with higher-stat units from those specific zones.
