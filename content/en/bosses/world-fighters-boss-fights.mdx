## Boss-by-Boss Strategy (Yuji, Gojo, Sukuna)

Knowing where bosses spawn is only half the work. The real gains come from minimizing deaths and maximizing uptime during each engagement.

### 1) Yuji-type spawn (upper bridge opener)
- Best as your first contact every loop.
- Great for warming cooldown rhythm.
- If your build has short-range burst, engage from side angles to reduce direct hit chains.

### 2) Gojo-type spawn (lower near)
- Often contested; arrive early and pre-position.
- Use mobility skills to maintain DPS while dodging heavy patterns.
- If you run in a group, assign one player as pull/control so others can free-hit.

### 3) Sukuna-type spawn (lower far)
- Treat as your “lap closer.”
- Save one major cooldown for this phase if your prior fights were clean.
- Don’t tunnel vision: surviving with 80% DPS beats dying with 100% intent.

| Boss | Threat Profile | Best Solo Approach | Best Team Approach | Priority Cooldown Use |
