## Mastering Unit and Gear Synergy

Units are the backbone of your damage output. As you move through different zones, the units you obtain grow stronger, but simply having a high-rarity unit is not enough. You need to focus on their passives and your equipment.

Your weapons are the most direct way to influence your damage output. If you have access to dual-wielding, prioritize this immediately, as it effectively doubles your potential DPS output. Accessories should be selected based on their specific percentage buffs to damage.

## Advanced Optimization Techniques

Once you have your core units and gear, you need to look at specific game mechanics that provide exponential gains. ## Maintaining Your Build Strategy

Consistency is vital. Many top players leave their accounts running overnight to farm gacha resources. Because the game relies heavily on rolling for mythical or secret units, you should never let your resources sit idle. 

1. **Auto-Click:** Use an auto-clicker to maintain constant DPS generation.
2. **Potion Stacking:** Always use Power and Damage potions simultaneously before engaging a boss.
3. **Awaken Routine:** Check your Awaken status regularly; resetting at the right time ensures your growth curve never flattens.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important stat for a World Fighters damage build?**

A: Power is the most critical stat. It dictates the rate at which your damage grows. The higher your power, the more effectively your damage multipliers (like potions and gear) will scale.

**Q: How do I get more swords for my build?**

A: You can obtain additional swords through the gacha system in different zones. If you have access to the dual-wield game pass, ensure you are equipping your two strongest swords simultaneously to maximize your damage output.

**Q: Are potions worth using for regular farming?**

A: It is generally better to save your strongest potions for boss encounters or high-HP enemies like "Shiny Cells." Using them for basic mob farming is often a waste of resources unless you are trying to push through a specific quest wall quickly.

**Q: How often should I use the Awaken feature?**

A: You should use the Awaken feature as soon as you hit the required power threshold. It resets your power but effectively doubles your potential, which is essential for scaling into the late-game zones.
