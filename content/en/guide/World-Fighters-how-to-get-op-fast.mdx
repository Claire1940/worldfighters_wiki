## World Fighters How to Get OP Fast: Passives and Accessories

To truly reach the upper echelons of power, you must optimize your passives and equip the right accessories. These items often provide percentage-based buffs that scale with your existing power, making them more valuable the stronger you get.

### The Divine Energy Passive
One of the most reliable ways to boost your character is through the Divine Energy passive. Unlike other gacha-based systems, you can guarantee this passive through a specific recipe. By combining **10 Wooden Katanas** and **6 Diamonds**, you have a 100% chance to obtain Divine Energy, which grants a 1.55x power multiplier. This is a "must-have" for any player looking at **World Fighters how to get op fast** strategies.

### Farming Boss Accessories
Accessories are dropped by secret bosses and mobs throughout the various worlds. These items occupy slots for the head, body, back, mask, and waist.
1. **Target Secret Bosses:** Every secret boss has a chance to drop a unique accessory.
2. **Event Game Modes:** Newer game modes often have higher drop rates for secret-tier accessories.
3. **Complete the Set:** Ensure you have an item in every slot to maximize the cumulative damage and power bonuses.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore the "Wanted" posters at the start of maps. Completing these rewards you with powerful characters like Ace, Katakuri, or Diablo, which act as massive power anchors for your team.

## Advanced Optimization: Titles, Swords, and Renaming

As you approach the level cap, small optimizations become the difference between being strong and being truly "OP." These final steps involve fine-tuning your equipment and utilizing lesser-known mechanics.

### Dual Swords vs. Power Pass
For players willing to spend currency, the choice of game passes is crucial. While a 2x Power pass is helpful, the **Dual Sword** pass is significantly more effective. Equipping two high-tier swords (like Yoru) can result in a 10x power multiplier, far outclassing the standard power doubler. For free-to-play players, the **Remote Stars** pass is the best investment, as it allows for efficient AFK farming of kills and drops.

### The Renaming Trick
A hidden gem in the optimization process is the renaming mechanic. By renaming your characters, you can effectively reroll their stat potential.
* **Target Stats:** Aim for at least a 1.6x to 1.75x multiplier on power and damage.
* **Method:** Repeatedly change the name (even using simple characters like "W") until the desired stats are achieved.

### Index Bonuses
Never underestimate the power of a completed Index. Every map has an index for the characters and items found within it. Collecting all characters in a set—especially reaching the Mythical tier—grants permanent damage and power bonuses that apply across all worlds.
