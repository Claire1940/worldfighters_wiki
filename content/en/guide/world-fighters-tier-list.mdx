## How to Win With (or Against) Top Tiers

A **world fighters tier list** is only valuable if it changes how you play. Here’s a practical approach for both sides of the matchup.

### Playing top tiers effectively

- Use their safe tools first; save risky options for hard reads  
- Take corner carry even if it lowers combo damage slightly  
- Rotate pressure timing to avoid predictable defense  

### Beating top tiers without switching mains

- Lab one guaranteed punish for each common safe string gap  
- Challenge movement habits, not just attacks  
- Use stage positioning to deny their preferred spacing  

| Top Tier Threat | Common Mistake Against It | Better Response |

| **Kage pressure snowball** | Panic reversal every turn | Delay tech + late mash on specific gaps |
| **Rook defensive wall** | Overextending after blocked strings | Back off, bait counter tools, punish recovery |
