## Mastering the Renaming System
One of the most significant quality-of-life additions is the unit renaming feature. This system allows you to customize your units while granting them specific power multipliers. By renaming your units, you can push their base stats beyond their natural limits.

To use this feature, navigate to the renaming interface, select your unit—such as a Shiny Diablo—and apply a new name. Early testing shows that specific names can trigger hidden multipliers. Always check your current power boost after renaming to ensure you have optimized your unit's performance.

## Tempest Invasion Game Mode
The Tempest Invasion is a 100-wave survival challenge that serves as the primary source of endgame currency. You will need a Slime Key to enter this mode. While it may seem daunting, the key to success lies in your positioning and your unit's attack range.

## Finding the Secret Boss
Hidden within the Tempest Federation is a secret boss that many players have yet to discover. Located in the back left-hand side of the map near the tri-colored building (yellow, green, and red), you can drop down into a hidden tunnel to initiate the encounter. This boss is the primary source for rare drops like Slime Wings and Demon Tokens.

For those looking to expand their collection, the [official Roblox game page](https://www.roblox.com) is the best place to check for additional community resources and patch notes.

## Maximizing AFK Progression
Since this update emphasizes long-term grinding, setting up an AFK farm is crucial. Ensure your mount is configured for ground movement to benefit from the new dash mechanics. By combining the new attack range upgrades with an optimized auto-clicker setup, you can farm tokens and experience while away from your keyboard.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check your inventory capacity before starting an AFK session. If your inventory fills up with common units, your auto-rolling will stop, wasting your valuable session time.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Slime Key for the Tempest Invasion?**

A: Slime Keys are obtained by defeating enemies within the Tempest Federation zone and as rare drops from the secret boss located in the hidden tunnel.

**Q: Does renaming a unit always increase its power?**

A: Yes, renaming provides a specific power multiplier. However, the exact boost amount can vary, so it is recommended to experiment with different names to find the highest possible buff for your specific unit.

**Q: Is the world fighters update 1 guide applicable to all platforms?**

A: Yes, the mechanics discussed in this world fighters update 1 guide, including the renaming system and Tempest Invasion, function identically across all supported platforms.

**Q: Where is the best place to AFK in the new update?**

A: The middle of the Tempest Invasion arena is the optimal spot, provided you have invested enough points into attack distance to hit enemies across the entire map.
