## Major Quality of Life Improvements

The 1.75 update is primarily a "Part 3" bridge leading into larger content expansions. While it may not introduce a dozen new worlds, it fixes several friction points that players have reported in previous weeks. One of the most significant additions is the **Auto Claim Rewards** feature, which allows players to collect their progression bonuses without manual clicking, though it is important to note that the rewards themselves do not currently auto-reset.

Another massive relief for the community is the inclusion of deletion confirmations. Players can now safely manage their inventory without the fear of accidentally deleting high-tier swords, units, or accessories.

| Feature | Impact on Gameplay | Priority for Players |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Auto Claim Rewards** | Removes the need for manual clicking in the rewards menu. | High |
| **Hide Gacha Animation** | Significantly speeds up the summoning process for mass pulls. | Essential for Grinders |
| **Delete Confirmation** | Prevents accidental loss of Secret and Mythic tier items. | Critical Safety |
| **Drop Billboards** | Displays potential drops directly next to game mode entrances. | Informational |
| **Last Position Teleport** | Returns you to your previous spot after finishing a game mode. | High |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep "Hide Gacha Animation" toggled on in the settings menu when spending large amounts of tokens to save time during long sessions.

## Speed Modifiers and Invasion Mechanics

The introduction of speed modifiers to Dragon Defense and Tempest Invasions is a game-changer for those looking to clear waves quickly. These modifiers allow the enemies to move and spawn at accelerated rates, which reduces the total time spent per run. However, these benefits come at a cost, requiring specific keys or premium currency depending on the multiplier level.

### Speed Multiplier Costs

Using these modifiers is the most effective way to farm tokens for your secret gachas. By doubling the speed of the waves, you can essentially double your hourly token income, provided your team is strong enough to handle the faster pace.

| Speed Multiplier | Resource Required | Recommended For |

| **1x Speed** | None (Default) | Standard progression |
| **2x Speed** | 1x Mode Key (e.g., Slime/Sayan Key) | High-efficiency farming |

## Combat Testing and the Damage Dummy

For the first time, players can accurately measure their DPS (Damage Per Second) without entering a live dungeon or raid. The new **Damage Test Dummy** is located directly in front of the leaderboards. This dummy has infinite health, allowing you to test different unit compositions, sword passives, and accessory combinations to see which setup yields the highest peak damage.

When testing your build, look for the "Peak DPS" number. For endgame players, hitting peaks around 70-80m is a solid benchmark for tackling the current hardest invasions solo. If your damage is lower, consider refining your accessories, as these are often the last items players max out to reach secret rarity.

## Advanced Farming Strategy: The "One Night" Method

The most efficient way to use the information in this **World Fighters update guide** is to master the AFK (Away From Keyboard) farming loop. With the fix to the "Last Position Teleport" bug, macroing game modes is now much more reliable.

1.  **Key Accumulation:** Spend time in the Dragon Arena or Slime Arena using an auto-clicker to farm as many keys as possible.
2.  **Auto-Invasion:** Set your preferred invasion (like Dragon Defense) to "Auto."
3.  **Speed Up:** Enable 2x speed using your accumulated keys to shorten the duration of each run.
4.  **Token Conversion:** After a night of AFK farming, you should have enough tokens to max out your secret gachas.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Ensure your inventory has enough space before starting an overnight session, or use the new auto-delete settings for lower-rarity items to prevent your vault from overflowing.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I use the 2x speed modifier in World Fighters?**

A: To enable 2x speed, you must enter a game mode like Dragon Defense or Slime Invasion and click the speed icon. It will prompt you to consume one corresponding key (e.g., a Sayan Key for Dragon Defense) to activate the boost for that session.

**Q: Where is the best place to test my damage?**

A: The developers added a dedicated Damage Test Dummy in the main hub, located right in front of the Leaderboards. It has infinite health and displays your current and peak DPS.

**Q: Does the Auto Claim Rewards feature reset automatically?**

A: No. While the feature will automatically claim rewards as you earn them during a session, you still need to manually reset the reward track once it is completed.

**Q: What is the most efficient way to get secret accessories?**

A: The most reliable method is farming the Slime Invasion. This mode drops items like the Admiral Cape and Fire Hat, which can be upgraded to Secret rarity. Using this **World Fighters update guide** strategy of 2x speed will help you cycle through these runs much faster.
